Cancellation Policy
Amanda Welch Podiatry operates a cancellation policy which is similar to many other medical clinics and we kindly ask all patients to adhere to it.
If you wish to cancel or reschedule an appointment we simply ask you to give a minimum of 24 hours notice.
If this minimum is not adhered to, we reserve the right to charge the full treatment cost of the appointment.

We are obviously aware that from time to time circumstances dictate that an appointment will be missed or less than the 24 hours notice will be given.
On such occasions we can be lenient but frequent missed appointments can be very disruptive to the smooth running of the clinic
and can also be inconvenient to other patients that require an appointment slot
